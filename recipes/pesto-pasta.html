Vegan Pesto Pasta Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

Description:

A delicious and easy-to-make vegan pasta dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 1 cup of fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup of pine nuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine the fresh basil, pine nuts, garlic, and nutritional yeast. Pulse until finely chopped.
  3. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until a smooth pesto sauce forms.
  4. Taste the pesto and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve your vegan pesto pasta hot, garnished with extra basil leaves if desired.

Enjoy your delicious homemade vegan pesto pasta!

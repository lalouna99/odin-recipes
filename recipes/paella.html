Paella

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

Description:

Indulge in a vibrant symphony of flavors with our Vegan Paella. Colorful bell peppers, tender courgettes, and aromatic spices dance alongside saffron-kissed rice, creating a masterpiece of Spanish cuisine. Each bite is a journey to sunny Mediterranean shores, a celebration of plant-based delight.

Ingredients

For the Vegetable Stock

  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves smashed
  • 1 carrot roughly chopped
  • 1 celery stalk roughly chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Paella:

  • 2 cups paella rice (short-grain rice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 3 gloves garlic minced
  • 1 red bell pepper thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper thinly sliced
  • 1 courgette/zucchini sliced into rounds
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoons thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh parsely chopped, for garnish
Instructions

Prepare the Vegetable Stock:

  • In a large pot, combine the vegetable broth, chopped onion, garlic, carrot, celery, bay leaf, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  • Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 20-30 minutes to infuse the flavors. Strain and set aside the vegetable stock.

Prepare the Paella

  • In a large paella pan or a wide, shallow skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.

Add Aromatics

  • Add the finely chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until it becomes translucent.
  • Stir in the minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.

Saute Vegetables

  • Add the sliced red, yellow, and green bell peppers, along with the courgette (zucchini). Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften.

Season and Add Rice:

  • Stir in the smoked paprika, saffron threads (if using), dried thyme, dried rosemary, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Add the paella rice and stir well to coat the rice with the flavorful mixture. Toast the rice for a couple of minutes.

Add Cherry Tomatoes

  • Add the halved cherry tomatoes and continue to cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Add Vegetable Stock

  • Pour in 4 cups of the prepared vegetable stock. Give it a gentle stir to distribute the ingredients evenly.

Simmer and Add Peas

  • Lower the heat to a simmer and let the paella cook for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is tender and has absorbed most of the liquid.
  • In the last 5 minutes of cooking, add the frozen peas.

Finish and Serve

  • Remove the paella from the heat and cover it with a clean kitchen towel or aluminum foil. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Enjoy your homemade vegan paella with the delicious combination of red peppers, courgettes, and other savory ingredients!
